About Us

Welcome to Isdaan ni Lynde!

At Isdaan ni Lynde, we are committed to providing the freshest seafood to the people of Cebu. With a focus on quality and sustainability, we source our seafood directly from the ocean, ensuring that every catch is fresh and of the highest standard.

Our mission is to connect our customers with the bounty of the sea, providing everything from fish to shellfish, and even cephalopods, all at the peak of freshness.

We pride ourselves on being a trusted supplier in the local community and hope to continue delivering delicious, sustainable seafood for years to come. Our goal is to offer you the best seafood, backed by our dedicated team and a passion for excellence.
